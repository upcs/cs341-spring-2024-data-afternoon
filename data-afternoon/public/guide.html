Home Services Volunteer Map Guide

Using Google Maps

This guide will help you understand how to use Google Maps to find locations and navigate.

Finding a Location

Enter the address or name of the place in the search bar. The map will zoom into the location.

Using Street View

Drag the pegman icon to a location on the map to see street-level imagery.

Getting Directions

Click on 'Directions', enter your starting point and destination. Choose your mode of travel to get step-by-step directions.

Exploring Nearby Places

Use the 'Nearby' feature to find restaurants, shops, and other places of interest around a specific location.
